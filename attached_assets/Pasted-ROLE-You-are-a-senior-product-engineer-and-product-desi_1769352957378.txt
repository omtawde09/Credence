ROLE:
You are a senior product engineer and product designer building a FinTech platform
for a hackathon.

You deeply understand wealth management, financial advisors, retail investors,
and regulated financial products.

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
"Design an interactive FinTech platform to manage a large network of financial
advisors and provide personalized investment guidance to retail investors."

The platform must:
- Efficiently organize and manage hundreds of financial advisors and portfolio managers
- Capture investor goals, risk appetite, and preferences
- Recommend suitable investment plans and advisory options
- Present recommendations in a clear, transparent, and trustworthy manner
- Support informed decision-making across the entire advisor–investor journey

OVERALL GOAL:
Build a complete demo web application that clearly solves the above problem end-to-end.

The app should demonstrate:
- Advisor management at scale
- Investor journey management (not just onboarding)
- Personalized, explainable investment guidance
- Trust, transparency, and suitability

This is a demo application for judges.
Clarity, reasoning, and UX matter more than backend complexity.

--------------------------------------------------
DESIGN & UX PRINCIPLES
--------------------------------------------------
- Professional FinTech product (Zerodha-style calm and minimal)
- Light background, clean typography
- Content-first, not flashy
- Trustworthy, regulated tone
- No crypto-style dashboards
- No excessive visuals or gimmicks

--------------------------------------------------
APP STRUCTURE (REQUIRED)
--------------------------------------------------
Build the app with the following core sections:

1. Investor Experience
2. Advisor Experience
3. Recommendation & Trust Layer

--------------------------------------------------
INVESTOR EXPERIENCE (MANDATORY)
--------------------------------------------------
Design an interactive investor journey that:

- Captures investor goals (retirement, wealth creation, home, education)
- Assesses risk appetite using scenarios, not jargon
- Understands time horizon and preferences
- Feels conversational and human, not like a long form

The investor journey should clearly show how the platform understands the user
before making any recommendation.

--------------------------------------------------
RECOMMENDATION & TRUST LAYER (CRITICAL)
--------------------------------------------------
Generate personalized investment recommendations that are:

- Goal-based
- Risk-aligned
- Preference-aware

For every recommendation:
- Clearly explain WHY it was suggested
- Show assumptions used
- Show best-case and worst-case outcomes (qualitative or mock data)
- Include disclaimers and suitability notes

Add trust features such as:
- Advisor–investor compatibility
- Fee and conflict-of-interest disclosures
- “What could go wrong?” explanations

This layer should make the platform feel transparent and defensible.

--------------------------------------------------
ADVISOR EXPERIENCE (MANDATORY)
--------------------------------------------------
Design an advisor-facing experience that demonstrates scalability:

- Advisor profile and expertise
- Ability to manage multiple clients
- Overview of active clients and assets (mock)
- Alerts for:
  - Risk mismatches
  - Reviews required after life events
- Quick client summaries for advisor preparation

The advisor side should feel like internal financial software:
functional, calm, and serious.

--------------------------------------------------
JOURNEY MANAGEMENT (KEY DIFFERENTIATOR)
--------------------------------------------------
Show that this platform manages the FULL journey, not one-time advice.

Include features such as:
- Life-event triggered re-planning
- Ongoing suitability monitoring
- Review prompts for advisors and investors

--------------------------------------------------
CONSTRAINTS
--------------------------------------------------
- Mock data is acceptable everywhere
- No real trading or payments
- No real market integrations
- Focus on reasoning, UX, and product clarity
- Do not overengineer

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL EXPECTATION
--------------------------------------------------
The final application should make it obvious to a judge that:
- The problem statement has been fully understood
- Both advisors and investors are served
- Recommendations are personalized AND explainable
- Trust and transparency are first-class concerns
- The platform can scale beyond a single user

If a feature does not directly support the problem statement,
do not include it.
