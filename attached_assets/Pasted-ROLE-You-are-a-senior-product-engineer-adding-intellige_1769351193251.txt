ROLE:
You are a senior product engineer adding intelligence and business logic
to an already-built FinTech web application.

IMPORTANT:
The UI is already finalized and should NOT be redesigned, restructured,
or visually altered in any way.

CONTEXT:
This is a hackathon demo for
"Intelligent Platform for Advisor & Investor Journey Management".

The application already includes:
- Investor onboarding
- Investment recommendation screen
- Advisor dashboard

Your task is to add meaningful product features and “wow” capabilities
that demonstrate intelligence, trust, and end-to-end journey management.

PRIORITY:
Feature depth and clarity > complexity.
Mock data and simulated logic are acceptable.

--------------------------------------------------
GLOBAL CONSTRAINTS (DO NOT VIOLATE)
--------------------------------------------------
- Do NOT change layout, colors, typography, or spacing
- Do NOT introduce new visual components unless strictly necessary
- Reuse existing UI patterns and sections
- Prefer text-first, explanation-driven features
- Keep everything professional and regulatory in tone

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE 1: EXPLAINABLE INVESTMENT RECOMMENDATION (CRITICAL)
--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
Make every investment recommendation transparent and defensible.

REQUIREMENTS:
For each recommended investment plan, generate a clear explanation covering:
- Investor goals
- Time horizon
- Risk tolerance
- Investment preferences
- Suitability reasoning

ADD:
- A collapsible “What could go wrong?” section
- Best-case and worst-case outcome explanation (textual)

RULES:
- No marketing language
- Plain, professional wording
- Feels like a financial advisory note

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE 2: ADVISOR–INVESTOR COMPATIBILITY SCORE
--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
Demonstrate intelligent matching between investors and advisors.

FEATURE:
Generate a mock compatibility score (percentage) based on:
- Alignment between investor goals and advisor expertise
- Risk philosophy match
- Experience with similar investor profiles

DISPLAY:
- Calm, non-gamified presentation
- Short explanation of contributing factors

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE 3: LIFE-EVENT TRIGGERED RE-PLANNING
--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
Show continuous journey management beyond initial onboarding.

FEATURE:
Support investor life events such as:
- Marriage
- Job change
- New child
- Home purchase

BEHAVIOR:
- When a life event occurs:
  - Notify that the financial plan requires review
  - Highlight which assumptions may be impacted
  - Suggest next steps (review, rebalance, advisor meeting)

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE 4: RISK MISMATCH DETECTION (ADVISOR SIDE)
--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
Help advisors proactively manage suitability risk.

FEATURE:
Detect when a client’s current portfolio risk deviates
from their stated risk profile.

BEHAVIOR:
- Flag client with “Risk Mismatch” alert
- Provide short explanation:
  - Current risk vs recommended risk
  - Suggested corrective action

LOGIC:
- Simple, mock thresholds are sufficient

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE 5: TRANSPARENCY & CONFLICT DISCLOSURE
--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
Build trust through explicit transparency.

FEATURE:
Display disclosures for:
- Advisor compensation model (mock)
- Fee impact explanation
- Conflict-of-interest statement

TONE:
- Neutral
- Regulatory-style
- Non-promotional

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE 6: ADVISOR COPILOT – CLIENT SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:
Improve advisor efficiency and readiness.

FEATURE:
Auto-generate a concise client summary including:
- Goals
- Risk profile
- Preferences
- Recent changes or alerts

FORMAT:
- Short paragraph
- Text-only
- Reused within advisor dashboard

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL INSTRUCTION
--------------------------------------------------
Focus on features that make the platform feel:
- Intelligent
- Trustworthy
- Continuous (not one-time advice)
- Enterprise-ready

Avoid overengineering.
Avoid visual embellishment.
Simulated logic is acceptable if the reasoning is clear.
